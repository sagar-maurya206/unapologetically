UNAPOLOGETICALLY ME..

Voice yourself unapologetically

Introduction


I am just another normal brown girl, surviving 19 years of life. I have always been a vulnerable kid englufed by the society. Being a survivor I advocate mental health, pen down my feelings, create safe spaces and voice against the wrong. Basically I do anything to everything whatever comes in my mind unapologetically.



SOS(Story of Survivors)


Each one of us has suffered at some point of time and has stories to tell. One way to de stigmatise mental health is to talk about it. So let's share our stories and empower everyone around us including our own self and let all know that we all are in this together.







Are you really well ??


Mental illness is usually invisible but that doesn't mean it doesn't exist. Majority of the people suffering from any mental illness do not even know that there's something wrong with them. So to just have a quick check up on some common symptoms and figure out if the feeling you are going through right now is just sadness or something different, take this quiz up.






Safe Space


This is your non judgemental space to vent all your emotions out. Feel free to scribble all the things which are bothering you. We are here to take all your worries.








Empathetic Ear


When you know you are not okay, and taking to a friend or writing it out is not helping. Dont't worry we are here for you to listen and guide you through the darkness. Get the appointment for Life/Personal/Relationalship/Career counselling in just one tap.
