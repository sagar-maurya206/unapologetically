Empathetic Ear

Don't worry we are here to listen and guide you



Sympathy is something you will get in abundance in the world. You will find millions who will let you down and feel sorry for that what you are going through but this is something we do not want. Mental illness is nowhere a sign of weakness, rather it certainly shows how strong you are because it is not easy to bottle up everything inside you and still conquer the world! Well you do not have to worry anymore, here we give you an empathetic ear to listen all you bottled up emotions. A helping hand from a survivor to a survivor. Feel comfortable to book a slot as per your convenience and seek help because it's a sign of bravery.
